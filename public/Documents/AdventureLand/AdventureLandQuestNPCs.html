Adventure Land Quest-Giving NPCs Guide

NPC Name Level Requirement Description
Daisy 5+ Assigns daily monster hunt quests, such as eliminating Cave Snakes.
Cue 10+ Provides quests related to item upgrading and combining.
Lucas 10+ Offers quests involving the use of stat scrolls for equipment enhancement.
Divian 10+ Guides players through setting up a merchant stand for trading.
Xyn 15+ Facilitates item exchange quests, allowing players to trade rare items.
Leo 20+ Introduces crafting quests, enabling players to create new items.
Ace 25+ Provides access to PvP-related quests and arena challenges.
Tristan 30+ Offers quests to exchange seashells for elixirs.
Wynifreed 35+ Handles quests for exchanging golden earrings.
Heathcliff 40+ Assigns quests to exchange gem fragments.
Smith 45+ Provides quests related to locking, sealing, and unlocking items.
Crun 50+ Issues the 'Licence to Kill' quest, granting access to special missions.
